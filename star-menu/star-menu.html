Starlight Café

Where every bite is a little wish come true.

Morning Constellations

  • Shooting Star Latte – Creamy vanilla latte topped with star-shaped cinnamon dust and whipped cream.
  • Milky Way Mocha – A blend of rich chocolate and espresso with galaxy sprinkles.
  • Comet Cappuccino – Espresso with steamed milk foam, lightly drizzled with honey.
  • Starlit Matcha – Whisked matcha green tea with sweet cream and edible shimmer.

Lunar Bites

  • Meteor Muffin – Fluffy blueberry muffin with a sugar crust that sparkles.
  • Cosmic Croissant – Buttery croissant filled with hazelnut chocolate.
  • Celestial Toast – Brioche toast topped with whipped ricotta, honey, and star sprinkles.

Sweet Nebulas

  • Galaxy Cake Slice – Marbled vanilla and chocolate cake with a galaxy glaze.
  • Moonbeam Macarons – Lavender, rose, and lemon pastel macarons.
  • Star Cluster Sundae – Vanilla ice cream, caramel drizzle, and mini candy stars.

Stellar Sips

  • Aurora Lemonade – Color-shifting lemonade that glows from pink to blue.
  • Nebula Smoothie – Mango, strawberry, and coconut milk swirls topped with mint.
  • Solar Sparkle Soda – Fizzy citrus soda with edible glitter and sugar rim.
